Zero2Maker

Zero2Maker Workshop was held at encube fablab, gandhinagar by Prof.Rajesh Nair. Many students of different ages and different regions had participated in the workshop.It was great workshop where students of age 5-20 worked together in a team.In the workshop we were taught how to innovate ideas and think in all direction for making a product and how to design it.

We were taught Arduino and little CAD.We were given task to design a working product using arduino for children within two days.Every team had done great job by designing different product. some had made remote control car,pinball, color detector and many other things.We had made a picky bank.At the end we were all thankfull to sir as we had learn lot of new things and experimented it.
